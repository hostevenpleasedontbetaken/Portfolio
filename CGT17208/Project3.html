DND Quest - Ideation

Initially, we created an ideation document. This document will be used throughout the entire project to document any ideas/research/etc.

Different from usual we did not start with researching everything at hand. When we first discussed the prompt we realized that it reminded us a lot of the game Pokemon Go. Since we were a group comprised of just game development majors we realized that the best option for us was to make a game involving our step counter prompt.

After brainstorming for a while we came to the conclusion of Dungeons and Dragons! We initially wanted to create an RPG of sorts as we know that there is an extremely large fanbase with this. The motivation behind players wanting to play our game is to continue to progress both through game aspects but also through their physical health. We were able to easily figure out our target audience, what we want to do with the project and the problem we are solving/our solution. Along with this, we designed a gameplay loop and gameplay systems.

From this point we started to do our research and created an interview template. We individually found information on the main competitor(Pokemon Go) and the effects that they have provided on their players through their game. Alongside this, we conducted interviews in the hope of understanding people with fitness watches at Purdue. We attempted to understand whether or not keeping track of their physical activity through devices affects their motivation when it comes to fitness along with in general how they feel about overall fitness.
